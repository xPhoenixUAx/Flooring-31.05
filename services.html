Planning notes

Good flooring starts below the visible surface.

  • Subfloor flatness and movement affect tile, hardwood, LVP, and laminate performance.
  • Moisture conditions should be reviewed before wood or basement flooring projects.
  • Transitions, stair edges, and base trim should be planned before installation begins.
  • Product selection should match traffic level, pets, cleaning habits, and room purpose.
  • Occupied homes and commercial spaces benefit from phased sequencing and dust control.
  • Manufacturer tolerances matter for warranty and long-term floor behavior.

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

Floens Flooring Services ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard information when you visit our website, submit a flooring service inquiry, request a quote, or communicate with us through the website.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By accessing or using our website, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use the website or submit information through it.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide to Us

We collect information that you voluntarily provide when you fill out contact forms, request a quote, ask about flooring services, send project details, communicate with us, or otherwise interact with the website.

This may include your name, email address, phone number, service address or general service area, preferred contact method, service interest, room type, approximate square footage, existing flooring, preferred materials, project timeline, photos, measurements, notes about subfloor issues, stairs, pets, moisture concerns, and any other information you choose to provide.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, certain information may be collected automatically about your device and browsing activity. This may include IP address, approximate location data, browser type and version, operating system, device information, pages visited, time spent on pages, referring website addresses, and general interaction data.

2.3 Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We may use cookies, web beacons, pixels, and similar tracking technologies to support website functionality, forms, analytics, performance monitoring, and advertising measurement. For more information, please review our Cookie Policy.

3. How We Use Your Information

We may use the information we collect to respond to inquiries, prepare flooring recommendations, schedule project reviews, communicate about services, provide estimates, coordinate project details, process payments where applicable, improve website usability, monitor website performance, prevent fraud or misuse, comply with legal obligations, and support business operations.

Where required by law, we will rely on your consent before using information for certain marketing communications. You may opt out of marketing communications where applicable.

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information with trusted service providers who help operate the website, manage communications, process forms, provide analytics, support advertising measurement, process payments, or deliver requested services.

We may also disclose information when required by law, to protect our rights or safety, in connection with a business transfer, or with your consent. Service providers are expected to protect information and use it only for the purposes for which it was shared.

5. Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect personal information. These may include secure servers, access controls, encryption where appropriate, monitoring, and administrative safeguards.

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of information submitted through the website.

6. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, or port certain personal information. You may also have the right to object to certain processing or opt out of certain marketing communications.

To exercise privacy rights, please contact us using the information below. We may need to verify your identity before responding to certain requests.

7. Children's Privacy

Our website is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us so we can take appropriate steps to delete the information.

8. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites, services, platforms, or resources. We are not responsible for the privacy practices, security, or content of external websites. Please review third-party privacy policies when visiting those websites.

9. California Privacy Rights

California residents may have additional rights under California privacy laws, including the right to know what personal information is collected, request deletion, correct certain information, opt out of certain sharing or sale where applicable, and not be discriminated against for exercising privacy rights.

We do not sell personal information as the term is commonly understood. If you are a California resident and wish to exercise applicable rights, please contact us using the information below.

10. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to, stored in, or processed in the United States or other countries where service providers operate. By using the website or submitting information, you understand that your information may be processed outside your country of residence where privacy laws may differ.

11. Data Retention

We retain personal information for as long as reasonably necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, respond to inquiries, maintain records, resolve disputes, comply with legal obligations, and support legitimate business purposes.

When information is no longer needed, we will take reasonable steps to delete, anonymize, or securely retain it according to applicable requirements.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will post the updated policy on this page and revise the "Last Updated" date where appropriate.
